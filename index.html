How-To Start A Story

  • Step 1: A New Story

    Press the start new story button. This will refresh the four cards.

  • Step 2: Character Cards

    Look at the top two cards, they're blue. These two cards make up your main character. This character is who your story is about.

  • Step 3: Plot Cards

    Now it's time to look at the next two cards, the green ones. These are your plot cards. Remember, every great story is about a character who wants something and has to overcome an obstacle to get it. One these cards should be your character want and the other one should be the obstacle. However, it's your story so you can take the plot anywhere you'd like.

  • Step 4: Write Your Story

    Once you have your character and your plot it's time to actually write your story. So open your favorite writting app and start writing.

  • Bonus Step: A Random Genre

    If you'd like to challenge yourself, once you have a story press the genre button to get a random genre as well.
